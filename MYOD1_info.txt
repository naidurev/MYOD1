Function:
---------
MYOD1 is a nuclear protein that belongs to the basic helix-loop-helix (bHLH) 
family of transcription factors. It acts as a master regulator of skeletal 
muscle differentiation and is capable of converting fibroblasts into myoblasts.

1. bHLH domain (basic helix-loop-helix)
