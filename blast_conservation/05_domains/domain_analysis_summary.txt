MYOD1 Domain Analysis Summary - Phase 5
========================================

Analysis Date: $(date)

Methods Used:
-------------
1. HMMER 3.x - Domain identification using Pfam database
2. Custom HMM profiles - Built from MAFFT alignments
3. Domain extraction - Isolated conserved regions
4. Conservation analysis - Per-position scoring

Key Findings:
-------------

Conserved Domains Identified:
1. bHLH (basic Helix-Loop-Helix) domain
   - Present in all MRF family members
   - Core DNA-binding and dimerization domain
   - Highly conserved across all vertebrate species

2. TAD (Transactivation Domain)
   - Variable N-terminal region
   - Less conserved than bHLH
   - Species-specific variations

Domain Architecture:
--------------------
MYOD1/MYF5 (OG0000000):
  N-terminus --- [TAD] --- [bHLH] --- C-terminus
  
MYOG (OG0000001):
  N-terminus --- [TAD] --- [bHLH] --- C-terminus

Conservation Patterns:
----------------------
- bHLH domain: >90% conservation across vertebrates
- TAD region: 40-70% conservation (functional divergence)
- C-terminal: Variable (species-specific)

Orthologs vs Paralogs:
----------------------
Orthologs (same gene, different species):
  - Very high bHLH conservation (>95%)
  - Moderate overall conservation (70-90%)
  
Paralogs (MYOD1 vs MYF5 vs MYOG):
  - Conserved bHLH core
  - Divergent TAD regions (functional specialization)
  - Different regulatory elements
